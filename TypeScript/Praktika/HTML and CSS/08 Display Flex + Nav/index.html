Flex

Container(tėvinis elementas)

  • display: flex;
  • flex direction: row(default) | column | row-reverse | column-reverse
  • flex-wrap: no-wrap(default) | wrap | wrap-reverse
  • flex-flow: flex-wrap flex-direction
  • justify-content: flex-start(default) | flex-end | center | space-between | space-evenly | space-around;
  • align-content :normal(default) | flex-start | flex-end | center | space-between | space-evenly | space-around | stretch;
  • align-items: flex-start | flex-end | center | stretch(default) | baseline
  • gap: row-gap | column-gape; (galima rasyt gap: *px *px)

Children

  • order: number; (default 0)
  • flex-grow: number (default 0);
  • flex-shrink: number; (default 1)
  • flex-basis: px | % (default - element size)
  • flex: flex-grow flex-shrink flex-basis (galima rašyt eiliŠkumu flex:)
